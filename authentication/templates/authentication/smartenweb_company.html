About Smarten Technology

SMARTEN is a professionally managed group and well equipped for working on projects related to national hydropower, telecommunications (like FTTA, FTTX, AMI Implementation, ERP) and construction along with necessary training, consultancy, and marketing services of various engineering products. We provide a complete solution for survey, site acquisition, installation, commissioning, and maintenance of all processes regarding Information & Communication Technology (ICT) infrastructures including power solutions and civil work in the field of telecommunications and its infrastructure.

Mission

SMARTEN aims to become one of the leading FTTA, FTTX, and AMI Implementation service providers in the country while maintaining the highest level of service quality. We strive to be our customers' preferred partner by providing appropriate, innovative, world-class, independent, dependable, affordable, and cost-effective solutions and services.

Vision

We at SMARTEN have a vision of evolving into a customer-focused company and becoming a leader in the field we are in by providing quality services to the full satisfaction of our customers and partners. Thus, we raise ourselves not only as a profit-oriented company, but also as company that builds an expert team that can be used in the development of society and nation.

Goals

The future is taking shape all around us. We will be able to see all kinds of differences over the next decade that we cannot even imagine today. By taking a proactive approach to meeting customer demands, SMARTEN will match the differences and contribute to the FTTA, FTTX, AMI, and ERP software for the timely and economical completion of the projects.
